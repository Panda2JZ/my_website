Privacy Policy - Finance Control

Finance Control is a personal finance management application developed by Panda 2JZ. The application works entirely offline and does not require an internet connection to function.

The app does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal or financial data. All data entered by the user is stored locally on the user’s own device and remains under their full responsibility and control.

Information Collection and Use

Finance Control does not collect any personal information, usage data, analytics, or device data. No information is sent to external servers or third parties. All features operate exclusively on the user’s device.

Disclosure of Information

As no user data is collected or transmitted, there is no information to disclose to third parties, service providers, advertisers, or authorities.

Analytics and Tracking

Finance Control does not use analytics tools, tracking systems, cookies, or third-party SDKs. The application does not monitor user behavior or collect anonymous usage statistics.

Opt-Out Rights

Since no data collection occurs, no opt-out actions are required. If you wish to stop using the application, you may uninstall it at any time using your device’s standard uninstall process.

Children’s Privacy

Finance Control does not target children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect any personal information from children. As no data collection occurs, there is no risk of storing children’s personal data.

Data Retention

The application does not retain or manage user data. All information remains stored locally on the user’s device until the user chooses to delete it or uninstall the app.

Security

Because Finance Control operates entirely offline and does not transmit data over the internet, no data is stored on external servers. Users are responsible for the security and backup of their own devices.

Your Consent

By using Finance Control, you acknowledge and agree that the application operates locally, does not collect data, and that all information entered is solely under your responsibility.
